- Minimum supersampling is retained where the performance budget permits, improving edge and text clarity on standard-DPR displays.
- Mobile transitions render the full-detail scene that matters visually and use the lighter representation only while the second environment is small.
- Mobile shadows use soft contact treatment; capable desktop devices retain PCF soft shadows.
- Live display textures, particles and route effects update at device-appropriate rates.
- Rendering pauses while the section or browser tab is not visible.
- WebGL context loss is handled without creating duplicate animation loops.
