CONNECTED 3D V50 — REBUILT FROM SCRATCH

Architecture
- New connected renderer written independently from the V43–V49 implementation.
- One service model is visible at a time; service models never overlap during the walkthrough.
- Models remain stationary. Scroll controls camera movement and chapter progression.
- Transitions use a short editorial camera retreat and dark cut, not moving or intersecting models.
- Stable render resolution; no motion/idle canvas reallocations during scroll.

Presentation

- Mobile safe framing gives the 3D model almost the full available scene width above the copy panel.
